Garlic Brown Sugar Glazed Salmon

Ingredients:
- 2 pounds salmon
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1/4 cup brown sugar
- 1/4 cup soy sauce
- 3 garlic cloves minced
- juice of one lemon
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp pepper
- garnish with sliced lemons and chopped parsley if desired

Recipe:
1. Preheat oven to 350º and line baking sheet with aluminum foil
2. Lay salmon on baking sheet and season with salt and pepper. Wrap sides of salmon with foil
3. In a small bowl, whisk olive oil, brown sugar, soy sauce, garlic, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Pour over salmon. Cover Salmon with aluminum foil
4. Bake salmon for 20-25 minutes
5. Take foil off of the top and baste the salmon with the sauce in the foil
6. Broil for 3-5 minutes, or until brown and caramelized
7. Add garnish if wanted
