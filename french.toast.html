French Toast

Ingredients:
- 1 egg
- 1 tsp pure vanilla extract
- 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
- 1/4 cup milk
- 4 slices bread
- Add whatever topping you want(s)

Recipe:
1. Combine all ingredients, except for bread, into bowl
2. Dip bread into mixture, evenly coating both sides
3. Cook bread on lightly greased nonstick pan until browned on both sides
4. Add toppings as desired
