Penne alla vodka

Ingredients:
- 1 lb penne
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 onion, chopped finely
- 3 tbsp butter
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 cup vodka
- 1 14 ounce can tomato purée
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 1 pinch red pepper flakes
- Salt and ground pepper

Recipe:
1. Cook pasta according to directions
2. Sauté garlic and onions in 2 tbsp of butter and olive oil in saucepan
3. Pour in vodka
4. Cook and reduce for 2-3 minutes, then add tomato purée. Stir until combined
5. Pour in heavy cream. Stir to combine
6. Add red pepper flakes
7. Stir in 1 tbsp butter
8. Drain pasta, and add into sauce. Toss to combine
