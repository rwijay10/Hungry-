Chicken Caesar Salad

Ingredients:
- 6 cups torn romaine, or lettuce of choice
- 1 lb boneless skinless chicken breasts, cooked and cut into strips
- 2 cups croutons
- 3/4 shredded Parmesan cheese
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 6 tbsp olive oil
- 1/3 cup lemon juice
- 3-4 minced garlic cloves
- 1/4 tsp coarse ground pepper

Recipe:
1. In salad bowl, coming first 5 ingredients (through salt)
2. In a small bowl, whisk oil, lemon juice, garlic, and pepper
3. Pour over salad and toss to coat
