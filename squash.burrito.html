Spaghetti Squash Burrito Bowl

Ingredients:
- 2 medium spaghetti squash
- Olive oil
- 1 15-ounce can black beans, rinsed and drained
- 1/2 cup corn kernels
- 1 tsp chili powder
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 2 bell peppers, sliced thinly
- 4 cups romaine (or lettuce of choice), chopped
- Add whatever topping(s) you want

Recipe:
1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Lightly grease a baking sheet.
2. Cut the squash in half. Drizzle a little oil on each half, then place flesh side down on the baking sheet. Bake for 40-45 minutes, until the squash are tender.
3. In a medium bowl, add black beans, corn, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper; stir to combine. Set aside
4. In a skillet, warm about 1 tablespoon olive oil. Add the sliced peppers and season with salt and pepper. Sauté for about 7 minutes
5. When the squash are done, stuff with romaine, black bean mix, and peppers. Top with desired toppings
