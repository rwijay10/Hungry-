Strawberry Banana Smoothie

Ingredients:
- 2 cups frozen strawberries
- 1 fresh banana, peeled
- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup ice
- 1 tsp honey, if needed to sweeten
- 1 tbsp peanut butter, if wanted


Recipe:
1. Add all ingredients to blender
2. Blend until combined, and serve
