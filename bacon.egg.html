Bacon, Egg & Cheese

Ingredients:
- 2 eggs
- Salt and pepper
- 3 tsp butter, room temperature
- 2 biscuits
- 2 slices cheese of choice
- 4 slices fully-cooked bacon

Recipe:
1. In a large nonstick skillet over medium heat, cook eggs and season with salt and pepper
2. Spread butter on biscuits, grill biscuits in wanted
3. Once eggs are almost finished, add cheese. Remove eggs once cheese is melted
4. Place eggs over biscuit and serve
