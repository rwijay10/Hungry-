Stir Fry

Ingredients:
Marinade:
- 2 tbsp unseasoned rice vinegar
-15 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp honey
-1 tbsp peeled/grated ginger
-1 tsp chili pepper flakes
- 1 tsp ground cumin

Meat & Stir Fry:
- 1 1/2 lb sirloin steak
- 1 tbsp cornstarch
- 2 tbsp vegetable/peanut oil
- 4 green onions
- 2 garlic cloves
- 3 sliced hot chilis
-1 inch ginger
- 1/2 cup chopped cilantro

Recipe:
1. Chill steak for 30 mins, slice into strips
2. Marinate beed in marinade
3. Mike cornstarch with 2 tbsp cold water to make a slurry
4. Brown the beef strips using oil in large sauté pan. Meat should be rare on inside
5. Once beef is cooked, stir fry the chiles, garlic, and ginger for 45 seconds
6. Add remaining ingredients and cornstarch slurry into pan and cook for 1 minute
